Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich

Description

This is a fun way to make an already tasty veggie even better.

Ingredients

  • Regular size carrots (Not baby carrots)
  • Spices of choice
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the carrots by peeling them and cutting off the stem
  2. Mix together spices and soy sauce
  3. Dip carrots in sauce
  4. Grill until crispy
  5. Serve and enjoy!
