Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This creamy dip is both spicy and delicious. It will be an absolute hit at your next event or even just an idea for dinner.

Ingredients

  • Shredded chicken, cooked or canned
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Cream cheese
  • Ranch dressing
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Heat a saucepan to medium heat
  2. Add chicken and cream cheese; stir until cream cheese is melted and combined fully with chicken
  3. Add buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, and hot sauce to taste
  4. Cook until hot and all Ingredients combined
  5. Serve with chips!
